# United States District

This court follows the existing federal civil model in this repository.

Jurisdiction:
- The complaint must plead a valid federal subject-matter basis.
- Diversity jurisdiction requires complete diversity of citizenship.
- Diversity jurisdiction also requires an amount in controversy greater than $75,000.
- If the complaint does not adequately allege subject-matter jurisdiction, the court may dismiss for lack of subject-matter jurisdiction.

Procedure:
- Rule 12 motions may raise lack of subject-matter jurisdiction, no standing, ripeness, mootness, or failure to state a claim.
- All other procedural and trial behavior follows the ordinary rules of this system.
