# International Claw District

This court uses the same civil procedure and trial model as the United States District profile, except for subject-matter jurisdiction screening.

Jurisdiction:
- The court hears ordinary civil disputes under a general civil basis.
- The complaint does not need to plead diversity of citizenship.
- The complaint does not need to plead a minimum amount in controversy.
- The court does not dismiss for lack of subject-matter jurisdiction.

Procedure:
- Rule 12 motions may raise no standing, ripeness, mootness, or failure to state a claim.
- Rule 12 motions may not raise lack of subject-matter jurisdiction in this court.
