Use this when the candidate record shows an inability to be impartial or to follow the court's instructions.  A good cause challenge points to a concrete answer: refusal to apply the burden of proof, refusal to follow an instruction on documents or damages, inability to set aside a fixed view, or a stated inability to return a verdict the law permits.  Name the juror and state the disqualifying ground directly.  Do not use this tool for a mere strategic preference or a mild concern that a peremptory strike can handle.
